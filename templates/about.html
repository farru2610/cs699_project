About WeatherFlow

WeatherFlow is your one-stop solution for accurate and real-time weather updates. Our app provides current weather conditions, including temperature, wind speed, and other important meteorological data. Our mission is to bring reliable weather information to your fingertips, making your life easier whether you're planning a trip or checking daily weather updates.

You can also download the dataset of the already searched cities which are stored in our database for other works (eg. ML related projects) which requires city wise temperature datasets.
